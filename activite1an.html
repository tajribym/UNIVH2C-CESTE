Integration Week for New Students

The CESTE club actively participated in the integration week for new students, organized by the Faculty of Sciences Ben M'Sik. The goal of this week is to present the different fields and facilitate the integration of new enrollees.

Dates and Location: October 12 and 13 at Amphitheater 8.

Activities: Theatrical play, choir, and poetry presented by members of the CESTE club. The participation also includes an orientation session led by the dean, vice-dean, and a presentation of the 14 faculty clubs.

Objectives: Orient new students and present the activities and opportunities offered by the CESTE club.
