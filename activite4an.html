Celebration of the Green March

The CESTE club organized a series of activities to commemorate the Green March, a Moroccan national day.

Date: November 18, 2023.

Location: Amphitheater 7

Activities: General presentation on the natural resources of the Moroccan Sahara by the earth sciences committee, a theatrical play about the Green March, "Aichi Yabaldi".

Objectives: To raise awareness among students about the historical importance and natural resources of the Moroccan Sahara.
