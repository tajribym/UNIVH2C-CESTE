Club Introduction


The Earth and Environmental Sciences Student Club (CESTE) was founded in 2007 by geology students from the Faculty of Sciences Ben M'Sik.

Its mission is to promote Earth and Environmental Sciences through various extracurricular activities, encompassing scientific, academic, and cultural aspects.
The club is primarily aimed at students of the faculty, providing them with a platform to enrich their knowledge and develop their skills in these fields.
The student in charge of the club for the 2023/2024 academic year is Mr. Ayoub Karroumi, and the faculty advisor for the club is Mr. Mustapha Mouflih.
