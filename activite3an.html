Museum Activities

The club prepared a museum within the faculty to exhibit species of geographical and temporal interest.

Dates: November 10, 2023

Location: Room 6 of the Faculty of Sciences Ben M'Sik.

Supervision: Mrs. Nadia Barhoun, Mrs. Ouariti from the Geology Department.

Activities: Collection of species of geographical interest (endemic) and on the geological time scale (trilobites, ammonites, fern traces).
Arrangement of animal bones and cleaning for presentation in showcases.

Objectives: Educate and raise awareness among students about geological and biological diversity, and promote the conservation of natural heritage.
